🚀 Why Machine Learning for Exoplanet Detection?

As space telescopes like Kepler and TESS collect massive volumes of stellar data, traditional methods of analyzing light curves and radial velocities are no longer efficient enough. Machine Learning (ML) has revolutionized this field by providing automated, accurate, and fast predictions.

🧠 Key Advantages of Using Machine Learning

  • Handles Big Data: Processes millions of light curves quickly.
  • Pattern Recognition: Detects complex transit patterns and distinguishes noise.
  • Reduces False Positives: Assigns probabilities to better prioritize follow-ups.
  • Time Efficiency: Saves months of manual data scanning.
  • Adaptive Learning: Improves as more labeled data becomes available.
